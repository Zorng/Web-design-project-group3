FAQs

Here's everything you may want to know. For any other info just reach us.
Can I use Coolors for free?
Yes. Coolors is always free to use for everyone. You can choose to upgrade to Coolors Pro for access to premium tools and content.
Does Coolors have any time-limits or trials?
Our free plan has no time-limits so you can use Coolors for as long as you need before deciding to upgrade.
Can I cancel or switch at any time?
Yes. You can manage your plan 24/7 in your account section. Downgrade to free plan will occur at the end of paid period.
What happens to saved palettes when downgrading to free?
Your content won't be deleted. Anyway, since the free plan has limitations in the number of palettes, projects and collections, in order to save new you'll need to delete older first.
How secure is the payment?
All the payments are powered by Paddle. Therefore your credit card details as well as billing info are stored securely on their servers.
Is the subscription shared between platforms?
Yes. By becoming a Pro user you will unlock all the features on any Coolors product like website, mobile apps and plugins.
